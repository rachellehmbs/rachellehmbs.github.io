About me

I am currently PhD student in the CRYPTO team at Université de Versailles Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines under the supervision of Christina Boura, Henri Gilbert (ANSSI), and Yann Rotella.

My main interest is symmetric cryptanalysis. During my PhD, I have worked on a variety of topics such as differential cryptanalysis of block ciphers, generic attacks on permutation-based AEAD modes, or algebraic attacks on reduced version of the hash function Keccak. I have also participated in the cryptanalysis of the AC2022 cipher Elisabeth-4.
